# Project Presentation Preparation Guide

This directory contains **SIMPLE** preparation materials for the **Margdarshan Career Helper** presentation.

## Easy Team Roles (Much Simpler!):

1. **Member 1 - Website Builder** (`member1_website_builder.md`) - Made the pretty website students use
2. **Member 2 - Data Manager** (`member2_data_manager.md`) - Handles saving and getting information
3. **Member 3 - AI Helper** (`member3_ai_helper.md`) - Makes the smart recommendations work
4. **Member 4 - Project Organizer** (`member4_project_organizer.md`) - Put everything together and made it work online

## Helper Files:

- **Simple Word List** (`simple_tech_words.md`) - Easy explanations of tech words
- **Easy Questions & Answers** (`simple_questions_answers.md`) - Questions teachers might ask with easy answers

## How to Study (Super Easy!):
- Read your part (just 10-15 minutes!)
- Look up any words you don't know in the word list
- Practice saying your part out loud
- Remember: You don't need to know everything, just your part!
